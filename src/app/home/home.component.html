What is Adaptive Examination?
• Adaptive examination is a method to conduct test that adapts to the candidate's capability.
• If a question answered is incorrect, the next question will be of a lesser difficulty level.
• If a question answered is correct, the next question will be of a higher difficulty level.
How the system works:
• Register yourself using an email.
• Login to the system.
• To enter questions to the database, click on Submit Questions.
• To give examination, click on Answer Test.
